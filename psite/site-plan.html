Overview

Purpose

We are currently in a great market for information technology professionals. Yet, would you know which steps to follow today communicate your strengths? You wouldn't be alone in wishing for a guide. This is the website I want to build. A website that includes the most up to date information on how to proceed during your job search. From resume to interviewing. From searching to determining whether a company would suit you. Would you be interested in such a resource?

Audience

My audience are all those wishing to further their careers while gaining a market perspective. Those who know can do better and have the learning skills but do not know how to push through the process. Also those who have successfuly achieved the desired job, but need a clearer view into what step follows.

Content

Home page

Wellcome to Ignite Jobs. We have a unique approach to jobsearch. Part of that stems from offering zero job listings or any related services. This page was born from from my frustration while trying to look for new jobs.

This page should be used as a workbook. Workbooks provide comprehensive steps towards developing an overall understanding of your current career hurdles. You will first be directed to look for a dream that can become true. Keep in mind that those can be either drastic changes or small incremental steps; the later is recomended and encouraged. Second comes the organization you'll need to develop while creating a plan. Don't worry, there is beauty in clutter too. And third, it's time to put yourself out there. You'll receive feedback from your first attempts to reach the job market. We'll used that in various iterations; meaning this won't be a one time thing.
