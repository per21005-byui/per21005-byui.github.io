Overview

Purpose

We are currently in a great market for information technology professionals. Yet, would you know which steps to follow today communicate your strengths? In my case I tend to be extremely nervous or act in an odd way during an interview. Because of that, a website that includes the most up to date information on how I gained experience and applied knowledge across my years at work would be ideal. Wouldn't it be a great resource?

Audience

My audience are both employers and recruiters seeking new talent to fill positions that require problem solving abilities and technical understanding. Also promote my ability to acquire new abilities and learn entire new development models on my own. The most interesting par would be a timeline where anyone could find a clear succesion of records on how experience was obtained.

Home page

Wellcome to my personal page where I keep a copy of my work experience for anyone interested and write about programming, technical support, industrial design and manufacturing and other stuff in various languages.

Who am I? I'm a personable and knowledgeable technical support representative with over 5 years of experience. Customer-focused, experienced in resolving software usage and configuration issues, as well as adept in finding teaching opportunities during support sessions. Passionate about fostering lasting professional interest in products and brands through cordial interactions with technical personnel.

At the moment I've got my sight fixed in doing a career switch. It means going from technical support in a software company servicing the aluminum manufacturing industry, to a more involved role in software development preferrably focusing on backend tasks. In this process I could leverage my technical support experience to first offer that experience and then seek moving closer to development opportunities. My return to study and personal interests and experiences also play an important role in this process.
